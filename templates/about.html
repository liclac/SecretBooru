About SecretBooru

SecretBooru is a 'Booru clone, that uses encryption to protect your pictures from prying eyes.

SecretBooru:

  • Encrypts every image you put into it
  • Gives every image a randomly generated encryption key
  • Stores encryption keys in an encrypted database
  • Does not "know" the correct password
  • Invalidates cookies on every launch

This basically means that nobody can read the stored image files, nobody can read the database (including things like tags).
It'll also be very difficult to steal your login, and it'd only work once.

The web interface is also designed to avoid revealing things - for example, tags are referred to by ID in URLs, rather than by name.

Disclaimer

I have very little experience when it comes to security.
SecretBooru will most certainly keep your sister out, but it most certainly won't keep any governments out.
